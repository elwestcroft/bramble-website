How It Works

Organize everything. Find anything. Stay in the story.

One home for it all

Characters, locations, arcs, and loot live in a Series Bible right next to the page. Capture something as soon as you write it, and revisit as often as you need.

Any fact in seconds

Travel time to the capital? Location of the cursed chalice? Who was the last suspect again? All accessible with a click of the cursor.

Momentum, built-in

Open your book and a "Previously On" card reminds you where you left off, plus any notes you left your future self. And then there's the sprints, goals, and quests to keep you motivated. You've got this!

Built for Big Stories

Series, sagas, and sprawling worlds aren't a problem anymore.

A Series Bible spans every book

Your series live on shelves with a bible that travels through each book. Pull characters into future instalments, easily search for answers. Stay ten steps ahead of those pesky readers looking for missteps.

Every arc, mapped beat by beat

Questions

Fair questions, straight answers.

What is Bramble?

Bramble is novel writing software that keeps characters, locations, storylines, and world rules organized in one place, beside the page. It is built for fantasy writers, LitRPG authors, series writers, and anyone whose story outgrew their tools.

Can I import my existing manuscript?

Yes. Bramble imports Word (.docx) files directly, so your book arrives with its chapters intact and zero copy-paste penance. Coming from Scrivener or Dabble? Export to Word first, then import.

What happens when the trial ends?

It ends. No card was taken, so nothing gets charged and nothing needs cancelling. Your work stays yours, and if you upgrade later it's all waiting where you left it.

What does "lifetime" mean in the Founder Edition?

The lifetime of Bramble's core product: every current and future core feature, one writer, forever. Test it first with the 14-day free trial, and if you buy and change your mind, our payment provider Lemon Squeezy offers a 30-day refund policy.

Is my writing private?

Your manuscript is your manuscript. Bramble is a writing tool, not an AI that trains on your book.

Does Bramble work offline? Is it Mac-only?

Yes. Bramble is a macOS app and your manuscripts are plain files on your Mac, so you can write on a plane, in a cabin, anywhere. We recommend keeping your library folder in iCloud, Dropbox, or Google for redundancy.

Legal

Privacy Policy

The short version. Bramble is a macOS app that runs on your computer. Your manuscripts, characters, notes, and everything else you create live as plain files on your Mac, not on our servers. We can't read your book. We don't want to. We don't train anything on it.

What Bramble stores, and where

Your projects are non-proprietary files on your disk. They stay there. Bramble does not upload your writing to us, and there is no Bramble cloud your manuscript is copied to. If you choose to keep your library in iCloud, Dropbox, or Google Drive, that is your storage provider under their terms, not ours.

What we do collect

Only what's needed to sell and license the app. When you buy Bramble or start a trial, our payment provider handles the transaction and we receive your email address and license status so we can support you and keep your license valid.

  1. Purchase and license data. Handled by Lemon Squeezy (or any successor payment provider) acting as merchant of record. They process your payment and billing details; we never see or store your full card information. We receive your email, order, and license key status.
  2. License validation. Bramble contacts the licensing service to confirm your license is active, with an offline grace period so you can keep writing without a connection. This exchanges your license key, not your writing.
  3. Update checks. Bramble checks for new versions so you get fixes and features.
  4. Optional support contact. If you email info@trybramble.app, we keep that correspondence to help you.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data. We don't use your writing to train AI. We don't run advertising trackers inside the app. There is no generative AI in your manuscript, by design.

Your choices

You can request a copy of the limited data we hold (your email, order, and license status), ask us to delete it, or deactivate a license from your device. Because your writing lives on your Mac, deleting your account with us does not touch your manuscripts; those are yours to keep or remove.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email info@trybramble.app. The full, current policy is published at https://trybramble.app/privacy.
